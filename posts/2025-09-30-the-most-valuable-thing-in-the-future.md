# The Most Valuable Thing in the Future

Date: September 30, 2025

We are launching a new approach to building products. This is a combination of new technology and a new mindset that makes it easy to create, share, and iterate on ideas.

This feels to many of us like a transformative moment, and it feels exciting and new. There is something great about making it really easy and fast to go from idea to result, and the new dynamics that emerge.

Innovation could be about to go through a significant acceleration, and along with it, the quality of products and experiences can drastically increase. Even in the very early days of exploring new tools, it's been striking to many of us how open the playing field suddenly feels.
